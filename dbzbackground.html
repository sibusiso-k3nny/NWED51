Dragon Ball Z Battle Of Gods

Summary: Dragon Ball Z: Battle of Gods is the first canonical Dragon Ball Z movie set after the defeat of Majin Buu. It introduces new powerful characters like Beruus The God of Destruction, Who came to earth to fight a Saiyan warrior called Goku, It expands the mythology of the gods in the Dragon Ball universe, and sets the stage for Dragon Ball Super.

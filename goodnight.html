Goodnight Mommy

Summary: Naomi Watts is the mother of boy’s twins, Cameron and Nicholas Crovetti. The twin brothers decided to go to their mother country home only for them to discover their mother face covered in bandages, her reason was she had a recent cosmetic surgery. They sensed something is wrong when their mother sets strange new house rules, she would do things she never liked or ever wished to do in her life, day by day boys suspicion were growing only to find out that the woman beneath the gauze, who’s making their food and sleeping in the next room, isn’t their mother at all.

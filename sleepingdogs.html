Sleeping Dogs

Summary: In this crime thriller, Russell Crowe stars as Roy Freeman, an ex-homicide detective with a fractured memory, forced to revisit a case he can't recall. As a man's life hangs in the balance on death row, Freeman must piece together the tragic evidence from a decade-old murder investigation, discovering a sinister web of buried secrets and betrayals linking to his past. With only instincts to trust, he faces a cold truth sometimes, it's best to let sleeping dogs lie.

Director: Adam Cooper

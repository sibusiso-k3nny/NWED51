About Us

CinemaPlus+ is a website based on movie reviewing,Its a place where you review your best movies from a limited Category.
Our website offers international movies from countries like United States,Japan,France and Italy , And many more. The Website was developed
by a group of excellent people called the Cybertrons, who are first years from Sol Plaatje University.


CinemaPlus+ Offers a limited catalogue of movies .There 10 categories of movies and each Catagory contains 8 movies. The categories

image website called Pinterest

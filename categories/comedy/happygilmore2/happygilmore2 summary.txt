Happy Gilmore 2
Comedy (2025)-Directed by Kyle Newacheck and written by Adam Sandler
Happy returns to professional golf to help pay for his daughter’s ballet school trip. Happy’s life has changed dramatically after accidentally killing his wife Virginia with a golf ball. He’s now an alcoholic working at a supermarket, his only motivation is to support his daughter’s dreams.

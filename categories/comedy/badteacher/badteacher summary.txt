Bad Teacher 
Comedy (2011)-Directed by Jake Kasdan and written by Lee Eisenberg and Gene Stupnitsky
A lazy and inappropriate middle school teacher Elizabeth Halsey who’s only goal is to marry a rich man and quit he job. When her fiancé dumps her, she sets her sights on a new target, Scott Delacorte, a handsome and wealthy substitute teacher.

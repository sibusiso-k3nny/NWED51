The movie follows Anna, a young woman who's abandoned her dreams of being a chef and is now drifting through her twenties with a series of bad choices. When Anna losses her house sitting job, he meets Matteo, a handsome Italian who has a villa in Tuscany, and decides to jet off to Italy.

Directed by Kat Coiro.

Main cast includes: Halle Bailey as Anna, Rege-Jean Page as Michael, Lorenzo de Moor as Matteo, Aziza Scott as Clair & Nia Vardalos as mrs Dunn.

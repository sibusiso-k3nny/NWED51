The story follows Poppy Wright and Alex Nilsen, two college friends who embark on summer vacation tradition together, despite living in different cities. As they navigate their differences and deepen their connection, they begin to question if their friendship could blossom into something more.

Directed by Brett Haley


Main characters are Emily Bader, Tom Blyth, Jameela Jamil, Lucien Laviscount, Sarah Catherine Hook.

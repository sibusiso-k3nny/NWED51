The story follows Lilly Bloom, a young woman who's rebuilding her life in Boston after tough past. She meets Ryle Kincael, a successful neurosurgeon with a trouble past. As they navigate their complicated feelings, Lily discovers a book that leads her to reconnect with her past in a way she never expected.

The movie follows Liam Page, a country music superstar played by Alex Roe, who returns to his hometown after a decade, hoping to win back his bride, Josie , played by Jessica Rothe, whom he left at the alter.

Directed by Bethany Ashtof Wolf


Main characters are: Alex Roe, Jessica Rothe, Abby Ryder Fortson, John Benjamin Hickey, Travis Tritt

#### SUMMARY

Angel Has Fallen was made in 2019 and is a political action thriller. Mike Banning, who is a Secret Service agent is framed for an attack that happened on the President, Allan Trumbull, he must now try and prove that he is not the one behind the attack. While he was trying to prove his innocence he uncovered that the attack goes deeper than he thought and that there is a traitor in the White House.



#### Main CAST
Director: Ric Roman Waugh

Mike Banning: Gerard Butler

President Allan Trumbull: Morgan Freeman

Cast also includes: Jada Pinkett Smith, Lance Reddick, Nick Nolte and Danny Huston

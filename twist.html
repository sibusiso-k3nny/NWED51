Twist

Summary: Set in modern-day London, Twist (2021) follows Twist, played by Raff Law, a skilled street artist and parkour runner dealing with the grief of losing his mother. He is drawn into a group of young criminals led by Michael Caine, who plays Fagin. The gang also includes Dodge (Rita Ora) and Red (Sophie Simnett), with whom Twist develops a romantic connection. Instead of traditional pickpocketing, this group operates as a modern street gang. In this version, Fagin plans an art heist as revenge against a wealthy and corrupt art dealer, played by David Walliams, who ruined his career.

Director: Martin Owen
